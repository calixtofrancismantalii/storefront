About Us

The care of patients with sleep apnea is disjointed, inefficient, slow and without capacity for monitoring and improvement.

MyApneaPath, provides a comprehensive, multidisciplinary, rapid, efficient, low cost, monitorable and adjustable sleep apnea care coordination system.

MyApneaPath also provides a care coordination platform that allows coherent, seamless, efficient, rapid, monitorable and adaptable platform among multiple stakeholders in the sleep apnea care continuum.
