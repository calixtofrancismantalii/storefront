HST PROGRAM

Verifies insurance and obtains prior authorization if needed
Informs ordering facility on patient eligibility and coverage
Retrieves raw data for scoring and sends overscored data to the sleep specialist
Receives final HST report and sends a copy to the ordering facility
Refer to DME service, Dental Sleep Medicine, Otolaryngology, Maxillofacial Surgery, Cardiology, Bariatric Surgery, Behavioral and Medical Weight Loss Program
Bills Primary Provider for Interpretation Fee and Equipment Use/Technical Support Fee
